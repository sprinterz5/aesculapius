Breakfast
Gluten-Free Pancakes with Maple Syrup and Fresh Fruit
Ингридиенты
  • 1 cup of gluten-free pancake mix
  • 3/4 cup of non-dairy milk
  • 2 tbsp of maple syrup
  • Fresh fruit (such as berries or sliced bananas)
Инструкции
  • In a mixing bowl, combine gluten-free pancake mix and non-dairy milk
  • Add in non-dairy milk and maple syrup, and mix until smooth.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with cooking spray or oil
  • Use a 1/4 cup measuring cup to scoop batter into the skillet.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  • Serve hot with maple syrup and fresh fruit.
Макронутриенты
  • Calories: 200 kcal
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 36g
  • Fat: 3g
Lunch
Quinoa Bowl with Roasted Vegetables and Chickpeas
Ингридиенты
  • 1/2 cup of quinoa
  • 1 cup of mixed roasted vegetables (such as sweet potatoes, Brussels sprouts, and carrots)
  • 1/2 cup of chickpeas
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Инструкции
  • Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  • Rinse quinoa and add it to a pot with 1 cup of water.
  • Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until all the water is absorbed.
  • In a mixing bowl, toss mixed roasted vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until tender and lightly browned.
  • Add cooked quinoa and chickpeas to a bowl.
  • Top with the roasted vegetables.
Макронутриенты
  • Calories: 155 kcal
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 24g
  • Fat: 4g

Dinner
Baked Salmon with Steamed Vegetables
Ингридиенты
  • 4 oz salmon fillet
  • 1 cup of mixed steamed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and snow peas)
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Инструкции
  • Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  • Season salmon filet with salt and pepper on both sides.
  • Drizzle olive oil on top of the salmon.
  • In a mixing bowl, toss mixed roasted vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Bake salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  • In a separate pot, steam mixed vegetables until tender.
  • Serve baked salmon with steamed vegetables.
Макронутриенты
  • Calories: 271 kcal
  • Protein: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Fat: 17g
